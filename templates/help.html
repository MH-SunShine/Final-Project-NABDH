Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, log in to your account, choose book appoitment in your dashboard , select your preferred doctor,choose an available time slot, and confirm your booking.

How can I access my medical records?

Your medical records are securely stored in your account. Log in and navigate to the "Medical Records" section to view or download your records.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use advanced encryption and security measures to protect your personal and medical information. All data is stored securely and only accessible to authorized personnel.

How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

You can cancel or reschedule appointments through your account dashboard. Go to "My Appointments" and select the appointment you wish to modify.
